Dimitar Vuksanov creates hand-printed graphics in an attempt to visualise glimpses and sensations of THE ALL, a metaphysical hyper-space where everything arises from.

Works begin in 3D, rendered through custom shaders that convert virtual objects and light paths into raster dots. These are translated into screen printing frames using photo emulsion and black light. The final stage is hand printing on various materials . Each of these transmutations create randomness and imperfections making every print unique.

He believes that randomness is where all complexity arises from, beginning in the mind.
THE ALL is mind. The universe is mental.
